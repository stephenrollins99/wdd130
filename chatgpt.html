About Gravity Falls

Gravity Falls is an American animated television series created by Alex Hirsch that aired on Disney Channel from 2012 to 2016. The show follows the adventures of twin siblings Dipper and Mabel Pines as they spend the summer with their great-uncle Stan in the mysterious town of Gravity Falls, Oregon. The series is known for its humor, mystery, and supernatural elements.
